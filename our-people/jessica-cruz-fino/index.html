Jessica Cruz Fino

Student Fellow 2021-22

Jessica Cruz Fino is a Colombian lawyer specialized in intellectual property, currently pursuing an LL.M. with a specialization in Competition, Innovation and Information Law at NYU. She is especially interested in entertainment law issues and cultural public policies. She earned her law degree from Universidad Externado de Colombia in 2018. Before coming to New York, she worked as an associate at a Colombian law firm in several matters related to corporate law, trademarks and copyright. She also worked as an in-house lawyer for a nonprofit in charge of the incentives provided by Colombian law to theaudiovisual industry.
