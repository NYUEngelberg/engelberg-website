GLAM-E Lab

The GLAM-E Lab is a joint initiative to work with smaller and less well-resourced UK and US cultural institutions and community organizations to build open access capacity and expertise.

Projects

GLAM-E Lab Building open access capacity and expertise at UK and US cultural institutions and community organizations.
ENRICH ENRICH supports the development of Indigenous based protocols and centered standard setting mechanisms, as well as machine-focused technology that inform policy, transform practices, & reform relationships between Indigenous communities and wider society.
Knowing Machines Knowing Machines is a research project tracing the histories, practices, and politics of how machine learning systems are trained to interpret the world.
Library Futures We tackle the cutting-edge issues around technology and access to information that other library organizations are slow to address, mobilizing a community of experts to encourage the adoption of technologies that uplift libraries in the digital age.
