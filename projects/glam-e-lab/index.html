Engelberg Center Project

GLAM-E Lab

Mathilde Pavis, Andrea Wallace, Michael Weinberg
Project Website

The GLAM-E Lab is a joint initiative between the Centre for Science, Culture and the Law at the University of Exeter and the Engelberg Center on Innovation Law & Policy at NYU Law to work with smaller and less well-resourced UK and US cultural institutions and community organizations to build open access capacity and expertise.

Students at both institutions work directly with cultural instituions as clients to develop internal and external policies to support open access programs. Those policies become part of the GLAM-E Lab playbook, that any institution can use to implement their own open access program.
