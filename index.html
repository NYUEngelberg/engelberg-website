Engelberg Center for Students

The Engelberg Center on Innovation Law & Policy provides a unique, interdisciplinary environment where scholars can examine the key drivers of innovation as well as the law and policy that best support innovation.

Students are involved in the Engelberg Center in a number of ways. They participate in events, engage with members of the Center, and work as fellows on research and projects. They can also take introductory and advanced courses on a range of topics that connect with the Centerâ€™s work.
