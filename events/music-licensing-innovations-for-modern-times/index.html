Music Licensing: Innovations for Modern Times

The conference assembled distinguished academics, industry representatives, and policy experts to discuss how the music licensing ecosystem can compensate the right people more transparently, fairly, and efficiently. Together with the Technology Policy Institute, Professor Christopher Sprigman organized this conference.
