January 22, 2020

Feeding a Baby Unicorn: What Startups Want From Their Lawyers

Please join the Engelberg Center, The Fourth Floor, and a panel of experienced startup attorneys as they discuss their experiences hiring, firing, and working with outside counsel. This is a rare opportunity for law firm lawyers, law students, and the tech community to hear directly from decision makers and understand the client view of startup law.

The Engelberg Center on Innovation Law & Policy at NYU School of Law provides a unique environment where scholars can examine the key drivers of innovation as well as the law and policy that best support innovation.
